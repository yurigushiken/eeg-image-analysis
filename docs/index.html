This website presents the findings and visualizations from our EEG study on numerical processing.

Use the navigation bar above to explore the different types of analysis results:

  • ERP Waveforms: Grand average event-related potentials.
  • Topomaps: Topographical maps showing the spatial distribution of EEG activity.
  • LORETA: Source localization results estimating the neural origins of the signals.
